Who we are

Bridging industry and policy for twenty-five years.

Sovereign Strategy spans the arc of corporate strategy and public policy — providing the adaptive advisory needed to navigate an ever-changing geopolitical landscape.

Grounded by a quarter-century of practice yet evolving with a relentless focus on the future, the firm remains committed to "doing things differently." We enable organisations to align commercial objectives with complex regulatory environments — for the benefit of industry, society and the global political sphere.

Our integrated framework synchronises public relations, public affairs and ESG. By synthesising brand identity, stakeholder management and strategic network building, we identify the commonality between public and private sectors that long-term policy outcomes depend on.

As a full-service agency, Sovereign provides comprehensive support across market access and business diplomacy, agile strategy and real-time insight, reputation and crisis advisory, global stakeholder engagement, and integrated communication campaigns.

Real-time insights and trackable performance indicators give clients the clarity to act with precision — consolidating communications into a cohesive identity valued worldwide.

Guided by integrity and accountability, we translate complex strategy into action: building trust, delivering outcomes and creating influence for the organisations we serve.

Our goal is to realise your aspirational vision.
Our passion is to implement the strategy that makes it happen.

ImpactOS · AI-powered platform

Impact data, analytics and reporting — automated.

ESG and social impact reporting is now engrained in business — critical for tendering, securing investment and tracking compliance against global standards. 08
Selected work

Outcomes, not activity.

01
FIND
Global health · 2020–2021

At the heart of the ACT-Accelerator

Built a new narrative resonating with the G20, the EU Commission and the WHO, and ran a 12-month networking, fundraising and profile-raising campaign. The result: FIND established as the "go-to" organisation on diagnostics, with direct fundraising engagement at heads-of-state and ministerial level to scale COVID-19 testing in low- and middle-income countries.

02
City of London
United Kingdom · multi-year

Securing Crossrail (the Elizabeth Line)

Retained to plan and deliver the advocacy strategy supporting Crossrail's development. The campaign framed the project as essential to London's pre-eminence in financial services — overcoming Treasury resistance and securing approval from the highest levels of government for one of the most consequential infrastructure projects in modern Europe.

03
Bloomberg
EMEA · 20+ years

Two decades of brand and policy across EMEA

Entrusted with promoting the Bloomberg brand across Europe, the Middle East and Africa for over twenty years. Advised on Green Finance, G20 engagement since 2017, and built relationships with the Saudi Finance Minister. Sovereign also arranges regular engagement with the UK Prime Minister, Chancellor and senior ministers.

04
KNPC, Kuwait
Middle East · CSR

The first audited CSR report in MENA oil

Provided strategic counsel to develop the Kuwait National Petroleum Company's first-ever CSR report compliant with international standards — and the first independently verifiable CSR report in the Gulf oil industry. Devised stakeholder engagement, infrastructure initiatives to reduce greenhouse gases, health and safety programmes, and community engagement.

05
Brazzaville Foundation
Africa · Global health

The Lomé Summit on falsified medicines

Designed the charity's organisational identity, then built a campaign tackling substandard and falsified medicines in Africa. Co-launched the "Medicines that Lie" Symposium with Harvard, the London School of Hygiene and Tropical Medicine and Wellcome Trust, then escalated to the 2020 Lomé Summit — a multinational campaign with seven African heads of state, attended by the Director General of the WHO.

06
Medicines for Malaria Venture
Multilateral · 10+ years

Partnership with the Islamic Development Bank

Aligned MMV's activities with the IsDB's Health Sector Policy, securing initial funding for late-stage drug development trials repurposing antimalarial compounds for COVID-19 therapeutics. Reinforced MMV's reputation as the pre-eminent malaria R&D organisation across the OIC countries.

07
Council for Heart Health
Global · NCD policy

A new global forum on cardiovascular disease

Set up the Council for Heart Health — bringing together clinical, academic and policy thought leaders, with founding support from Novartis and the International Pharmaceutical Federation. Targeted UN SDG 3.4 on premature NCD mortality, with a new website, Politico-led communications campaign and presence at multilateral fora.

08
D-Wave
Europe · Quantum technology

Pan-European quantum computing launch

Used Sovereign's networks to organise the European launch of D-Wave's first quantum computer outside North America, at Forschungszentrum Jülich. Secured speakers including the European Commissioner for Innovation, the German Federal Minister of Education and Minister-Presidents of North Rhine-Westphalia, plus media coverage in Politico and Handelsblatt.

09
Unitaid
Global health · UK presence

Building UK presence and narrative

Built a comprehensive engagement strategy, identifying the right levers for change to raise awareness of Unitaid's work and create alignment between government stakeholders and Unitaid. Helped develop a memorable narrative and presence in the UK, generating positive engagement with Ministers and MPs.

10
Wolters Kluwer
Global · Tax policy

Global tax policy intelligence

Provides Wolters Kluwer with the most important tax policy updates from across the world, three times a week. Constant monitoring of global governmental policy that may affect tax — supporting a group serving customers in over 180 countries with operations in over 40.

11
Sanofi
France · Community engagement

Community engagement programme

Faced with hostility from a local MP and a residents' campaign attempting to ban deliveries of low-level radiated waste for hospital treatment, Sovereign organised a comprehensive community engagement initiative including retired persons groups, local schools and regular open days — defusing the campaign and protecting facility operations.

12
London Midland Rail
UK · Customer satisfaction

Politicians, opinion formers and customer satisfaction

Engaged politicians, opinion formers and stakeholders to develop the operator's understanding of public perception of its rail services. The work formed the basis for constant dialogue with policy-makers and the travelling public — and contributed to London Midland winning the UK Customer Satisfaction Awards 2017.
